A collection of courses I am currently teaching (or was teaching not long ago or will be teaching very soon).

Computer Application of Statistics
------
A one-semester lab course in the Bachelor's program in Psychology, which I have been teaching at the [University of Graz](https://www.uni-graz.at/en/){:target="_blank"} in each summer semester since 2022 (typically two groups with about 25 students in each group).

Introduction to Bayesian Statistics: Basics and Application
------
A two-day workshop serving as an introduction to the fundamentals of Bayesian inference statistics and data analysis with a strong focus on numerical modeling and simulation. Offered by the Graz Center for Empirical Research (GMZ), and held together with my colleague and friend in statistics and many other nerdy things Hanna Rajh-Weber. More information available [here](https://grazer-methodenkompetenzzentrum.uni-graz.at/en/workshops/registration-for-members-of-the-university-of-graz/?esraSoftIdva=717447){:target="_blank"}.
